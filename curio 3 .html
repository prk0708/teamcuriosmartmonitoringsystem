Location Overview

Comprehensive overview of all monitoring locations across Northeast India. Each location is equipped with sensors that continuously track water quality parameters.

Key metrics monitored at each location:

  • pH levels and turbidity
  • Bacterial contamination indicators
  • Chemical composition analysis
  • Temperature and environmental factors

Real-time data from these locations helps identify contamination sources and prevent disease outbreaks.

Water Quality Analysis

Detailed analysis of water quality parameters with threshold-based alerting system. Our advanced sensors detect even minor changes in water composition.

Parameters monitored:

  • pH Levels: Optimal range 6.5-8.5
  • Turbidity: Measured in NTU, alerts above 5 NTU
  • Chlorine Residual: Maintained at 0.2-0.5 mg/L
  • E.coli Count: Zero tolerance policy

Automated alerts trigger when any parameter exceeds safe limits, enabling immediate response.

Pathogen Detection System

Advanced pathogen detection using DNA-based analysis and machine learning algorithms. Our system identifies harmful microorganisms before they cause outbreaks.

Pathogens monitored:

  • Vibrio cholerae (Cholera)
  • Salmonella typhi (Typhoid)
  • Hepatitis A virus
  • E.coli strains
  • Giardia and Cryptosporidium

Early detection allows for targeted interventions and prevents widespread disease transmission.
